The Dad & Daughter 5K is a fun, family friendly event
for all runners and walkers.

Our 5K race is a family-oriented event, designed to be enjoyed by participants of all ages. Whether you're an experienced runner, a first-time participant, a walker, or simply looking to enjoy a fun day out with your family, our race has something for everyone.


We encourage families to participate together and create lasting memories. We have a variety of activities, arts and crafts, games, and entertainment for families to enjoy, making it a fun day out for everyone.


We take pride in creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, where families can bond over the shared experience of running and supporting each other. So, grab your family and friends and join us for a day of fun, fitness, and community!

Join Our 5K Run/walk for a Good Cause

Get ready to run or walk for a good cause!
Our 5K run/walk is coming up soon and we would love for you to join us.

  • Date and Time

    The 2025 run will take place in North Kansas City on Saturday, September 27th at 8:30 A.M.

  • Cost

    The registration fee is $25 per person until June 1st, after which the price will increase. All proceeds will be donated Pheonix Family.

  • Awards

    Every participant gets a t-shirt and a finishers medal. Overall winners for male/female category and the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, place winners for each age group will receive coupons to Dick's Sporting Goods.

  • What is allowed?

    We want to make sure that everyone who wants to participate can. We allow runners, walkers, strollers, baby joggers, small wagons, crutches, and wheelchairs.

  • Festivities

    The event is filled with fun festivities for children such as craft making, face painting, hula hoop contest and a Zumba warm-up as well as running.

  • Food

    We'll have hot and fresh cheese pizza, breakfast bars, chocolate milk, fresh fruit, donuts, and wings. Perfect for a quick energy boost before you run, or refuiling your body with carbohydrates and protein after the race.
